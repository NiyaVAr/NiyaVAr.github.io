Her Secret Recipe

Grandma Archuleta has been developing and experimenting with her secret recipe since her childhood in the early 70's. Since then, she's modified and learned from the people and culture around her, picking up new tips and tricks until she finally developed her breath taking recipe today. Enjoy flavorful meat and well seasoned dough.It's a twist on a classic mexican dish; a comfort food in many households.

There are many flavors! Try:

  • Chicken
  • Beef
  • Pork
  • Jalapeno Cheese
